Movie Review

Govindhudu Andarivadalae Movie Review:












Rating:

  • The movie got good rating .
  • Audience satisfied and gave good rating tthe movie.

Plus points:

  • Memorable family entertainer movie
  • Family emotions and human values is simply superb.
  • All actors done superb job for their roles.
  • Bringing back the missing family values and human emotions on screen.

Minus points:

  • First half of the film is a bit slow, when compared to the second.
  • If the makers would have added some comedy, it would have been more entertaining for the audience.
  • Story of the film is quite old and is predictable most of the times.
